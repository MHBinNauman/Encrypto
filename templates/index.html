Welcome to our Cryptographic Toolset

An application designed to simplify the world of encryption and decryption. Whether you're looking to secure your messages or dive deeper into cryptography, this application offers easy-to-use tools for both learning and practical use.

Our app features two powerful cryptographic methods: the Generalized Caesar Cipher and RSA Encryption. The Generalized Caesar Cipher allows you to encrypt and decrypt messages by manipulating text with two parameters: Scaling factor and Shifting factor. For those interested in breaking codes, we even offer frequency analysis, which helps decode messages without needing the original key. On the other hand, RSA Encryption empowers you to generate public and private key pairs for secure communication, giving you a solid foundation in modern encryption techniques.

The application is intuitive and user-friendly. To encrypt a message, simply choose your cipher, enter your text, and let the app handle the rest. Decrypting is just as easy — just input your encrypted message and key parameters. And if you’re working with RSA, generating keys and using them for encryption or decryption is just a click away. Whether you're new to cryptography or need a tool for secure communication, this application is here to help. Start encrypting today and explore the fascinating world of cryptography!
